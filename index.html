About VagCare

VagCare.in is an educational platform created to promote evidence-based sexual health awareness for adults aged 18 and above. Our mission is to provide reliable, research-backed information about sexually transmitted infections (STIs), reproductive health, contraception methods, and safe practices.

We aim to reduce misinformation by offering structured educational content that encourages responsible decision-making and promotes overall well-being.

Self Assessment Tool

Our interactive self-assessment tool allows adults to answer structured sexual health-related questions. Based on your responses, you will receive an educational risk indication and guidance.

  • Contraception and Family Planning
